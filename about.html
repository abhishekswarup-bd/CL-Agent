Our Story

From Challenge to Solution

TestMagicks was founded by a group of passionate educators who witnessed firsthand the struggles of coaching institutes—manually creating hundreds of mock tests was exhausting, time-consuming, and often resulted in inconsistent quality.

We understood the Indian competitive exam ecosystem deeply. Whether it's CAT, CLAT, IPMAT, or any other high-stakes exam, students deserve access to expertly crafted, varied practice material that truly prepares them for success.

That insight led us to build TestMagicks—an AI-powered platform that generates unlimited, high-quality mock tests and study material, freeing educators to focus on what they do best: teaching and mentoring students.

📚

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Democratize high-quality test prep content by leveraging AI to empower educators and students everywhere. We believe that access to excellent practice material should never be a barrier to success.
