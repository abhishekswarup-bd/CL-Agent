Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between monthly and annual billing? ▼

Yes! You can switch your billing cycle anytime from your account settings. If you upgrade from monthly to annual, we'll credit the unused portion of your monthly subscription toward the annual plan.

Is there a free trial for paid plans? ▼

Absolutely! All Pro and Institute plans come with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period. Cancel anytime if you're not satisfied.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets like Google Pay and PhonePe. All transactions are processed securely through our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime without any penalties or hidden charges. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing cycle. No questions asked!

Do you offer discounts for bulk licenses? ▼

Yes! For organizations looking to purchase multiple Institute plans or custom packages, we offer special volume discounts. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs and get a tailored quote.
