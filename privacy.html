1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide:

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, organization name, and password. For institutional accounts, we may collect additional information about your organization and role.

Content Data

We collect and store the mock exams, slide decks, and other content you generate using Career Launcher. This includes the exam configurations, questions, answers, and explanations you create.

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your interactions with our platform, including the features you use, content you generate, and time spent on various tasks.

Technical Data

We collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, operating system, referring pages, and pages visited. This helps us understand how our platform is used and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our Career Launcher platform and services
  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing payments and managing subscriptions
  • Sending service-related notifications and support communications
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Detecting and preventing fraud and unauthorized access
  • Complying with legal obligations and resolving disputes
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Conducting research and analytics to improve our services

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with multiple layers of protection. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Employee training on data privacy and security

While we employ strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

4. Third-Party Services

Career Launcher integrates with third-party services to provide enhanced functionality:

AI Providers

We use AI services from providers like Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and Groq to generate content. Your content data may be processed by these providers to generate high-quality mock exams and learning materials.

Payment Processing

Payments are processed through Razorpay. We do not store your complete payment card information. Razorpay maintains PCI DSS compliance for secure payment processing.

Analytics

We use third-party analytics services to understand how our platform is used. These services may collect information about your interaction with the platform.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and login information
  • Understand how you use our platform
  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Track usage patterns for analytics

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the platform.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object: Opt-out of certain uses of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent: Revoke consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@careerlauncher.com with your request and proof of identity.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 6 months after termination
  • Content data: Retained until you delete it or your account is closed
  • Usage logs: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics and security purposes
  • Payment data: Retained as required by law for 7 years for tax and compliance purposes

8. Children's Privacy

Career Launcher is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

For educational institutions, we implement age-appropriate privacy controls to protect student data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of Career Launcher after changes constitute your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@careerlauncher.com
  • Address: Career Launcher Technologies Pvt. Ltd., New Delhi, India
  • Response time: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 7 business days
